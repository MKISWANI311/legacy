Flexibility
Not only you can safely store your private data in the system but you can also fully customize it. There are only general templates for the start, everything further you can change or reorganize according to your taste or necessity.

How it works
All the magic is in your browser. It's aBlackBox - everything is encrypted and decrypted right in the browser and to the server is just a mess data sending. So nobody can see your data except you having the password.
